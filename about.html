MEGHA SOUHARDHA CREDIT CO-OPERATIVE LTD. BALLARI

Regd. Under Karnataka Souharda Co-Operative Act – Reg No.4101/2015-16

Megha Souhardha Credit Co-Operative Ltd. is, in every sense, a truly SOUHARDHA credit co-operative society. Megha started its operations in the year 2015 as a credit co-operative society primarily catering to the local masses in Ballari District who came majorly from the rural background. With a mission to uplift the weaker sections of the society from the rural areas.

Our Souharda has a duly elected board of directors. Our Souharda is a family of honest loyal and committed professional harmoniously employing the new technology to achieve the Members delight & human touch, which is the cornerstone of the success & the focus of all the efforts. Our Souharda is focusing on for healthy sustainable long-term growth.

Our Souharda is fully computerized from the day one The Member can have one to one service without having to shuffle between different tables. Every staff is well trained in all the area so has to provide the Members needed service on the spot at Doorstep. We are giving different types of Savings Schemes to our Members for Financial needs.

VISION OF MEGHA SOUHARDHA

To make financial services universally available to promote poverty alleviation through the development of a strong, sustainable economic infrastructure using financially sound market mechanisms

MISSION

➤ Linking the promotion of sustainable livelihoods with income-generation activities for the community
➤ Ensure that all members follow the democratic way of making policies and electing representatives and have an equal voice
➤ Ensure that all profits are controlled democratically by members and for their benefit
➤ Enhance accessibility of financial services

OBJECTIVES

➤ Inculcate good habits of savings in the cooperative members
➤ Focus on poor, low-income and deprived people of the community, especially women who have no accessibility to any financial institutions
➤ Provide opportunities to people to organize services collectively by pooling their resources without depending upon the government or other agencies
➤ Provide education, training and information to develop their members as well as their staff
➤ Enhance women’s participation and decision in policy making-processes at all levels
